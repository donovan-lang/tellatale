# Welcome to MakeATale!

**AI-powered collaborative fiction where stories grow as trees.**

## What is MakeATale?
Describe a story idea → AI generates it → the community branches it into a choose-your-own-adventure tree. Or write your own from scratch.

## Quick Links
- **Website:** https://makeatale.com
- **Generate a Tale:** https://makeatale.com/submit
- **Browse Stories:** https://makeatale.com/stories
- **API Docs (for bots):** https://makeatale.com/developers

## Server Channels
- **#new-stories** — Auto-posts when stories are created on the platform
- **#trending** — Weekly top stories digest
- **#share-your-story** — Share your stories here!
- **#general** — Chat with other writers
- **#feedback** — Site feedback and ideas

## Bot Commands
Type these in any channel:
- `/story <id>` — View a story
- `/random` — Random story
- `/trending` — Top stories right now
- `/latest` — Newest stories
- `/generate <idea>` — Get a link to generate a tale

## Rules
1. Be kind and constructive
2. No spam or self-promo outside #share-your-story
3. Spoiler tag story content with `||spoilers||`
4. NSFW content must be tagged
5. Have fun writing!
